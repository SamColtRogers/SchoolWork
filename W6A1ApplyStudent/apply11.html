How to Build Good Credit

The processs of building credit goes with first responsibility and secondly management. The first step is to review all finances such as bills, debt, and income. Second step is to create a budget that prioritizes bills then debt. What ever is left can be used in percentage in both savings and debt. The third step is to follow through with the budget.
